FAQ's

What banking channels does Zemen Bank offer to its customers?

Zemen Bank offers a variety of banking channels, including ATM Banking, Internet Banking, and Mobile Banking. These channels provide customers with convenient ways to access and manage their accounts, tailored to their preferences and needs.

What services are available through Zemen Bank's Internet Banking platform?

Zemen Bank's Internet Banking platform offers a range of services to customers, including the ability to view account balances, download account statements, transfer funds to other banks, monitor loan progress, access daily currency exchange rates, and request cheque services, among other functionalities.

What is the Zemen Platinum Travel Prepaid Mastercard and how does it benefit customers?

The Zemen Platinum Travel Prepaid Mastercard is Ethiopia's first prepaid and contactless travel card, offered in partnership with Mastercard. This innovative card allows Ethiopian travelers to conveniently carry USD or EUR currencies without the need for cash. With contactless features, it ensures safety and convenience during overseas travel, reflecting Zemen Bank's commitment to introducing cutting-edge banking technologies for customer safety and convenience.
